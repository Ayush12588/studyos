Why Streaks Work

The Science Behind Showing Up Every Day

Habit research shows that students who study for 45 minutes daily for 60 days outperform students who cram before exams. BoardOS Study Streaks are built on this science.

  • Consistency compounds — 60 days of 45 minutes beats 3 days of 6 hours
  • Visual progress makes you feel invested — you won't want to break the chain
  • Milestone rewards give you goals during long-term preparation
  • Streak data helps you identify your weakest study days and adjust them

FAQ

Questions About Study Streaks

What is a study streak in BoardOS?
A study streak is the number of consecutive days you've completed a study session in BoardOS. Each day you study, your streak grows. Missing a day resets the counter — which motivates you to show up every day.
What counts as a study session to maintain a streak?
Any meaningful study activity in BoardOS counts — reviewing formulas, completing notes, or logging a timed study block. You set the minimum daily goal, and we track whether you've hit it.
Do I get rewards for long study streaks?
Yes! BoardOS awards bonus XP and special badges at streak milestones — 7 days, 30 days, 60 days, and beyond. Your streak also contributes to your overall level in the Rewards & XP system.
What happens if I miss a day?
Your streak resets to zero, but your total XP and progress are never lost. BoardOS shows your longest streak as a badge of honour, and you can start a new streak immediately.
How does a daily study streak help with CBSE board exam preparation?
Research shows that consistent daily practice is far more effective than cramming before exams. A 60-day streak before CBSE boards means 60 days of spaced repetition, steady revision, and compounding progress — the foundation of board exam success.
