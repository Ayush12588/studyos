FAQs

Daily Tasks Questions Answered

How is StudyOS different from a regular to-do app for studying?

Unlike generic to-do apps, StudyOS links your tasks directly to your study plan, subjects, and chapters. Completing a task updates your overall CBSE preparation progress automatically.

Can I set recurring daily study tasks in StudyOS?

Yes. You can set daily recurring tasks like 'Revise 20 formulae' or 'Solve 1 past paper question set' that appear automatically every day without manual re-entry.

What happens to tasks I don't complete today?

Incomplete tasks roll over to your backlog and are flagged for next-day attention. StudyOS ensures nothing slips through the cracks.

Can I prioritise tasks by subject or urgency?

Yes. You can tag tasks by subject, add priority labels, and sort your daily list by urgency, estimated time, or subject weight.

Does StudyOS suggest what tasks to do each day?

Yes. Based on your study plan, exam countdown, and backlog, StudyOS generates a recommended daily task list every morning.
