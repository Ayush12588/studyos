FAQ

Quiz Generator — Your Questions Answered

How does the StudyOS Quiz Generator work?

You select a CBSE subject, class, and chapter. StudyOS AI instantly generates a set of practice questions — MCQs, short answers, or assertion-reason format — aligned to CBSE board patterns. No manual question creation needed.

Are the quiz questions aligned to CBSE board exam patterns?

Yes. All questions are generated to match CBSE board question formats including MCQs (1 mark), short answer (2–3 marks), long answer (5 marks), and assertion-reason questions used since 2020.

Can I generate quizzes for any CBSE subject?

Yes. StudyOS Quiz Generator supports all major CBSE subjects for Classes 9–12: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Social Science, English, Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies, and more.

Does the quiz system track my performance over time?

Yes. Every quiz result is saved to your profile. The system tracks your accuracy per chapter over time, identifies weak areas, and feeds this data to your AI Coach for personalised study recommendations.

Can I retake the same quiz or get new questions each time?

The AI generates a fresh set of questions each time you start a quiz on the same chapter, so you always get new practice material. You can also review past quiz attempts.
