FAQs

Session Logger Questions

How does StudyOS track my study sessions?

You start a session from the StudyOS dashboard, select your subject and chapter, and start the built-in timer. When done, you log notes, rate your focus, and save. StudyOS stores every session in your history.

Can I log sessions after the fact without using the timer?

Yes. StudyOS allows manual session logging — you enter the subject, chapter, start time, end time, and any notes.

Does StudyOS show me how many hours I've studied per subject?

Yes. Your session history automatically aggregates into subject-wise study hour totals. You can view this by day, week, month, or across your full CBSE preparation period.

Can I use StudyOS like a Pomodoro timer?

Yes. StudyOS includes a Pomodoro-style session mode — 25-minute focused work intervals with 5-minute break reminders. Sessions are logged automatically after each cycle.

How does session logging help with CBSE exam preparation?

Most students overestimate how much they study. Session logging gives you hard data so you can see which subjects need more attention and rebalance your preparation accordingly.
