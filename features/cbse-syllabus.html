FAQ

Common Questions About the CBSE Syllabus Tracker

Does StudyOS have the full CBSE syllabus pre-loaded?

Yes. StudyOS includes the complete CBSE syllabus for Classes 9, 10, 11, and 12 across all major streams — Science, Commerce, and Arts. Every chapter, unit, and topic is pre-mapped so you can start tracking progress immediately.

Can I track syllabus completion subject-wise?

Absolutely. The CBSE Syllabus Tracker shows completion percentages for each subject separately, and you can drill down to individual chapters and sub-topics.

How does the syllabus tracker help with board exam preparation?

The tracker calculates how much syllabus remains versus how many days are left before your board exam, then flags high-risk topics that need urgent attention.

Is the CBSE syllabus updated every year in StudyOS?

Yes. The StudyOS team reviews and updates the CBSE syllabus at the start of each academic year to reflect the latest NCERT curriculum changes and board updates.

Can I mark topics as 'needs revision' separately from 'done'?

Yes. Each topic can be marked as Not Started, In Progress, Done, or Needs Revision — giving you a granular view of where you actually stand.
