FAQ

Exam Countdown — Your Questions Answered

How does the BoardOS Exam Countdown work?

You enter your CBSE exam dates for each subject. BoardOS displays a live countdown in days, hours, and minutes, and calculates how many topics you need to cover per day to complete your syllabus before the exam.

Can I add multiple CBSE exam dates for different subjects?

Yes. You can add separate exam dates for each CBSE subject — Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Biology, English, etc. BoardOS shows a countdown and readiness status for each one individually.

Does the countdown adjust my study plan automatically?

Yes. As your exam date approaches and your remaining syllabus is tracked, BoardOS dynamically adjusts your daily study targets and revision priorities to ensure you cover everything in time.

Will I get alerts if I'm falling behind schedule?

Yes. BoardOS sends urgency notifications when your study pace falls behind what's needed to complete the syllabus before your exam date. These alerts are based on real progress data, not guesswork.

Is the CBSE exam date calendar pre-loaded?

BoardOS publishes CBSE board exam date sheets when officially released by CBSE, and you can also enter custom dates. Pre-loaded dates are updated every academic year.
