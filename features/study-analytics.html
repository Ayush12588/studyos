FAQ

Analytics Dashboard — Your Questions Answered

What data does the StudyOS Analytics Dashboard show?

The dashboard shows total study hours by subject, daily and weekly study patterns, quiz accuracy trends, revision completion rates, syllabus progress percentages, streak history, and an overall board exam readiness score.

Can the analytics help me identify which subjects I'm neglecting?

Yes. StudyOS compares time spent across all subjects and flags imbalances — for example, if you've logged 10 hours of Maths but only 2 hours of Chemistry over the same week.

How is study time tracked in StudyOS?

Study time is tracked automatically via the Focus Timer. Every time you start a focus session and link it to a subject/chapter, the time is logged. Manual entries are also possible.

Can I share my analytics report with my parents or teacher?

Yes. You can export a weekly or monthly analytics report as a PDF or shareable link. It shows study hours, quiz scores, and progress — great for parent-teacher meetings.

Does the dashboard show me my best study times?

Yes. StudyOS analyses when during the day you complete the most sessions and perform best on quizzes, then suggests these as your peak study hours for scheduling future sessions.
