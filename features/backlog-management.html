FAQs

Backlog Manager Questions

What is a study backlog and why does it matter?

A study backlog is the accumulation of chapters and tasks that were planned but not completed. For CBSE students, an unchecked backlog leads to syllabus gaps that directly hurt board exam scores.

How does BoardOS automatically detect backlog items?

Any task or chapter that passes its scheduled date without being marked complete is automatically moved to your backlog with the date it was missed.

Can I manually add items to my backlog?

Yes. You can manually add chapters or topics you know you've missed — even from before you started using BoardOS.

How does BoardOS help me prioritise my backlog?

BoardOS sorts your backlog by days overdue, CBSE exam weightage, and subject urgency. High-weightage chapters that are most overdue appear at the top.

Will clearing my backlog affect my study streak?

Yes — positively! Every backlog item you clear contributes to your daily completion score and earns XP. Maintaining a clean backlog is one of the fastest ways to build a strong study streak.
