FAQ

Questions About Rewards & XP

How do I earn XP in StudyOS?
You earn XP for completing study sessions, reviewing formulas in the library, maintaining your daily streak, hitting streak milestones, adding personal notes, and completing weekly challenges. The more consistently you study, the more XP you earn.
What can I do with XP and rewards in StudyOS?
XP unlocks new levels on your student profile and fills your rewards bar. When your rewards bar fills, you earn rewards like Streak Freezes, profile themes, exclusive badges, and early access to new features. Higher levels also unlock XP multipliers.
Is the reward system just for fun, or does it actually help with studying?
Both. The reward system makes studying feel genuinely enjoyable, which is proven to increase study frequency and session length. But more importantly — to earn XP, you actually have to study. Every XP point is a real action taken toward your CBSE preparation.
Does the XP system work across all subjects?
Yes. You earn XP for study activity in any subject — Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Biology, or any other CBSE subject. Weekly challenges sometimes focus on specific subjects to encourage balanced preparation.
Can I see how my XP compares to other CBSE students?
StudyOS includes an optional leaderboard where you can compare your XP and level with friends or your class. You can set your profile to private if you prefer to track progress personally.
